spirit flame

Spirit Flame is the very first skill Ori learns, and one of only two skills obtained through a Story Event and not an Ancestral Tree. Learned when Ori discovers Sein in the Sunken Glades, Spirit Flame allows Ori to fire Sein's light at an enemy. Provided certain abilities are present, it can hit multiple enemies at once.

wall jump

Wall Jump is the second skill Ori learns, and the first skill to be had from an Ancestral Tree. Passed on to Ori by the light of Fil, it allows the young spirit to jump up and off of walls, and enables access to areas that cannot be reached by a single jump alone. It is essential to exploring Nibel. The Ancestral Tree for Wall Jump is found deep within the Sunken Glades.

charge flame

Charge Flame is the third skill Ori learns, and the second skill to be had from an Ancestral Tree. Passed on to Ori by the light of Fil, it allows the young spirit to charge Sein's light and release it in a large explosion. It is essential to defeating certain enemies and destroying certain obstacles. The Ancestral Tree for Charge Flame is found in the Hollow Grove.

double jump

Double Jump is the fourth skill Ori learns. Ori receives this skill from Leru's Ancestral Tree, tucked away in Gumo's Hideout. It allows Ori to Jump again while already airborne, or jump twice (hence the name). The second aerial jump produces a characteristic "V" on screen which indicates that Ori has taken a double jump. This skill is required to continue exploring Nibel after Gumo's Hideout, and the extra jump also makes many pickups accessible that otherwise weren't.

bash

Bash is the fifth skill Ori learns. Ori receives this skill from the Ancestral Tree of the Spirit Tree, located in the Thornfelt Swamp. It allows Ori to project himself off of enemies, enemy projectiles, and lanterns. It is essential to solving many puzzles and accessing many areas. It is also essential to defeating certain enemies.

stomp

Stomp is the sixth skill Ori learns. Ori receives this skill from the Ancestral Tree of the Spirit Tree, located in the Thornfelt Swamp. It allows Ori to obliterate certain obstacles and enemies by slamming into the ground. It is essential to solving many puzzles and accessing many areas. It is also essential to defeating certain enemies.

kuro's feather

Kuro's Feather is the seventh skill Ori learns. Ori receives this skill from the Ancestral Tree of the Spirit Tree, located in the Thornfelt Swamp. It allows Ori to glide through the air, which is essential to solving many puzzles and accessing many areas. It is also essential to defeating certain enemies.

climb

Climb is the eighth skill Ori learns. Ori receives this skill from the Ancestral Tree of the Spirit Tree, located in the Thornfelt Swamp. It allows Ori to climb up walls, which is essential to solving many puzzles and accessing many areas.

charge jump

Charge Jump is the ninth skill Ori learns. Ori receives this skill from the Ancestral Tree of the Spirit Tree, located in the Thornfelt Swamp. It allows Ori to jump higher than usual, which is essential to solving many puzzles and accessing many areas.

light burst

Light Burst is the tenth skill Ori learns. Ori receives this skill from the Ancestral Tree of the Spirit Tree, located in the Thornfelt Swamp. It allows Ori to fire a burst of light in any direction, which is essential to solving many puzzles and accessing many areas. It is also essential to defeating certain enemies.

dash

Dash is the eleventh skill Ori learns. Ori receives this skill from the Ancestral Tree of the Spirit Tree, located in the Thornfelt Swamp. It allows Ori to move quickly in any direction, which is essential to solving many puzzles and accessing many areas.
